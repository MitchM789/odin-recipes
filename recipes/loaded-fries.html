Caramelised onion and chorizo loaded fries

Get a load of these fully sick fully loaded caramelised onion and chorizo loaded fries

For cheesy goodness, tuck into these full loaded fries topped with baked with chorizo and caramelised onion.


Ingredients

  • 1kg pkt frozen straight-cut chips
  • 2 red onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 chorizo, halved, thinly sliced
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 1/2 cups (165g) Devondale 3 Cheese Blend
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 shallot, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp drained jalapenos, finely chopped
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1/4 cup coriander leaves

  1. Preheat oven to 220C. Line a large baking tray with baking paper. Spread chips over tray and bake for 25 mins or until golden and crispy.
  2. Meanwhile, heat a large non-stick frying pan over high heat. Cook onion and chorizo, stirring occasionally, for 10 mins or until golden. Add sugar and vinegar and cook, stirring, for 1 min.
  3. Sprinkle chips with half the cheese. Top with the onion mixture. Sprinkle with remaining cheese. Bake for 5 mins or until cheese melts and is golden.
